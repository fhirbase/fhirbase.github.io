Why FHIRbase?

Why choose FHIRbase for your next Health IT project?



FHIR-native system

If you are working on FHIR-system from scratch FHIRbase provides you with a solid persistence layer:

  • Battle-proven & flexible design
  • Transactional consistency (ACID)
  • Ability to store FHIR resources as is in JSONB
  • Power and Expressiveness of SQL
  • Advanced indexing for search performance

Analytics on FHIR

If you have a bunch of FHIR data and want to derive meaningful insights from it, you can use FHIRbase as an analytical tool:

  • Load your data
  • Perform analytical queries with SQL
  • Leverage PostgreSQL ecosystem
