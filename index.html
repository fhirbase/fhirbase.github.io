Why fhirbase?

Why to choose FHIRbase for your next Health IT project?



FHIR-native system

If you working on FHIR-system from scratch FHIR-base provide you with a solid persistence layer:

  • Battle-prooved & flexible design
  • Transactional consistency (ACID)
  • Store FHIR resources as is in JSONB
  • Power and Expressiveness of SQL
  • Advanced indexing for search performance

Analytic on FHIR

If you have bunch of FHIR data and want to understand it you can use fhirbase as analytical tool

  • Load your data
  • Do analytical queries with SQL
  • Use PostgreSQL ecosystem
